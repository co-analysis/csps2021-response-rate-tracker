Final CSPS 2021 Response Rates

The Civil Service People Survey 2021 has closed and final response rates have been confirmed. In total there were 327,388 responses, this is an overall response rate of 62.1%.

  5. Some organisations invite a larger number of people working for their organisation than their official civil service headcount to complete their survey. For example, FCDO ask locally engaged staff working in overseas missions and MOD ask military staff working in civilian MOD roles to participate in the survey.
